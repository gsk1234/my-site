About Us

SWIFT MFI is one of the world's largest youth operated micro‑finance institutions. Since our inception in 2019 as a student entity, we have now expanded to impact borrowers in over 77+ countries with the help of our partners! Today we are proud to say that we have lent interest‑free loans to over 3200+ women and families who often live below the world poverty line, impacting over 74,000 lives.

Learn more about our work by exploring the site or getting in touch with our team.

What We Do

Supporting Female Entrepreneurs

At SWIFT, we are dedicated to stepping up our efforts in ending financial inequality. Support is by no means an easy feat; we believe we can facilitate progress in this area through community empowerment.

Educating the Community

Our goal is to promote great opportunities for those in need. With access to the right resources, people can become empowered by their own abilities and gain the confidence to fulfill their potential.
