About Us

SWIFT MFI is one of the world's largest youth operated micro-finance institutions. Since our inception in 2017 we have empowered thousands of women entrepreneurs to escape poverty through micro-loans, mentorship and training.

Learn more about our work by exploring the site or getting in touch with our team.

Meet Our Team

What We Do

Supporting female entrepreneurs

We provide micro-loans to young women enabling them to start or expand small businesses. Our support gives them the confidence and capital to turn ideas into reality.

Educating the community

Our workshops and trainings educate youth about microfinance and financial literacy. By building awareness we are creating a culture of entrepreneurship and empowerment.
