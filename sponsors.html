Our Sponsors

National Bank of Oman

The National Bank of Oman (NBO) has been a critical supporter of our micro‑finance initiatives. As one of the leading banks in Oman, NBO’s commitment to corporate social responsibility is demonstrated through their sustained partnership with SWIFT, helping us reach more women entrepreneurs across the region.

Mohsin Haider Darwish LLC

Mohsin Haider Darwish is one of the largest privately owned business houses in the Gulf region. Their diverse portfolio—from automotive to technology—speaks to their spirit of innovation. Their sponsorship enables us to scale our programs and provide interest‑free loans to even more families.

Zawawi Group

A respected Omani business group investing in sectors such as manufacturing, engineering and hospitality. The Zawawi Group’s dedication to social development aligns closely with SWIFT’s mission, and together we foster opportunities for women to start sustainable enterprises.

Al Habib & Co

As a leading real‑estate firm in Oman, Al Habib & Co offers a one‑stop shop for property development and investment. Their partnership with SWIFT helps provide the financial backing required for our growing list of community projects.
