Our Awards

At SWIFTmfi, each award we receive is more than just a trophy; it’s a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and the communities we serve. These accolades reflect the hard work, dedication, and passion of our team in delivering outstanding financial solutions and services.

United Nations – Volunteer Award 2022

The V‑Awards are a prestigious recognition for young volunteers making impactful contributions to societal and developmental challenges. In 2022, our founder, Gobhanu Korisepati, received this honour from an esteemed group of presenters: Shri Anurag Thakur, India’s Minister of Sports & Youth Affairs; Shombi Sharp, United Nations Resident Coordinator; Toily Kurbanov, Deputy Executive Coordinator of the UN Volunteers programme; Shoko Noda, UNDP Resident Representative in India; and Shalina Miah, UN Volunteers Regional Manager for Asia and the Pacific【260811372046738†L40-L49】.

Diana Award

The Diana Award stands as a beacon of recognition for young individuals who drive positive change within their communities and beyond. Named in honour of Princess Diana, the award celebrates those who embody her belief in the power of young people to change the world. It is presented to exceptional youths who inspire and mobilise their peers, engage in community service, and foster social change【260811372046738†L53-L64】.

Asia Book of Records

The Asia Book of Records celebrates extraordinary achievements across the continent. It recognises those who push the boundaries of possibility.
